About The Competition

What is the Zenova CBT Competition?

The Zenova Mega CBT Quiz Competition is an educational online competition created to help students prepare effectively for JAMB and other major examinations using a real CBT environment.

Participants will answer carefully selected questions across core science subjects under timed conditions while competing with top students nationwide.

Guidelines

Competition Rules

All participants are expected to follow these rules to ensure a fair competition for everyone.

1
Participants must not cheat, copy, or use unauthorised materials during the competition.
2
Multiple accounts are strictly prohibited. Each student may only register once using their unique access code.
3
Ensure a stable internet connection before starting the exam to avoid disruptions.
4
Competition timing must be strictly followed. Late logins may result in disqualification.
5
Any form of malpractice or misconduct may lead to immediate disqualification.
6
All participants must submit their answers before the timer runs out to be ranked.
